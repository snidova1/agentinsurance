## 🎯 PROBLEM & SOLUTION

### The Problem
AI agents now manage $4.2B+ in capital across DeFi, trading, and autonomous workflows. When they fail (and they do — flash crashes, prompt injection, oracle errors, data leaks), there's zero coverage. Traditional insurance can't price agent risk. Manual claims take weeks. By the time payout arrives, the protocol is dead.

### The Solution
AgentInsurance is parametric insurance designed for AI:
- **Smart contract triggers** — losses verified on-chain by oracle network
- **Instant payouts** — USDC sent in 60 seconds, not 60 days
- **AI-native risk scoring** — premiums based on agent behavior data
- **Multi-chain** — Solana, Ethereum, and EVM L2s
- **Stablecoin native** — USDC for premiums and payouts (perfect for stablecoin thesis)
